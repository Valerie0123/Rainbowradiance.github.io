About Us

car washing and detailing

Rainbow Radiance Carwash are dedicated to providing top-notch care for your vehicle, inside and out. With our comprehensive range of services, including seat washing, vacuum cleaning, interior wet cleaning, and window wiping, we ensure that every inch of your car receives the attention it deserves. Our team of skilled professionals utilizes advanced techniques and premium products to rejuvenate your vehicle's interior, leaving it spotless and fresh. Whether you're looking to remove stubborn stains, eliminate odors, or simply enhance the overall cleanliness and comfort of your car, trust Rainbow Radiance to deliver exceptional results.

What Do We Offer?

Washing Services

Exterior Washing

Our exterior washing service provides a thorough cleanse, removing dirt, grime, and debris to reveal a sparkling finish that enhances the appearance of your vehicle while protecting its paintwork.

Interior Washing

Our interior washing service ensures a pristine cabin environment by meticulously cleaning all surfaces, including dashboards, door panels, and upholstery, leaving your car's interior looking and feeling fresh and inviting.

Vacuum Cleaning

Say goodbye to crumbs, dirt, and pet hair with our vacuum cleaning service, which targets every corner and crevice of your vehicle's interior to achieve a clean and tidy result.

Seats Washing

Restore comfort and cleanliness with our seats washing service, which effectively removes stains, spills, and odors from your car's upholstery, leaving it fresh and inviting.

Window Wiping

Our window wiping service guarantees streak-free clarity by meticulously cleaning both the interior and exterior surfaces of your car's windows, providing optimal visibility for safer driving.

Wet Cleaning

Experience a deep cleanse with our wet cleaning service, specially designed to tackle stubborn stains, spills, and odors, leaving your vehicle's interior looking and smelling like new.

Oil Changing

Keep your engine running smoothly with our oil changing service, where our skilled technicians replace old, worn-out oil with high-quality lubricants to ensure optimal performance and engine longevity.

Brake Reparing

Ensure your safety on the road with our brake repairing service, where our experienced technicians diagnose and address any issues with your vehicle's braking system, restoring confidence in your car's ability to stop effectively.
